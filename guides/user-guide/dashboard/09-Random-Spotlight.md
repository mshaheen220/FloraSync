# Random Spotlight & Fun Facts

Learn while you grow! Enjoy a rotating card that surfaces smart tips—like helpful pruning reminders or companion planting warnings—alongside fun botanical trivia based entirely on the specific plants you are currently growing.

### How It Works
Every time you open your dashboard, FloraSync picks a plant from your active garden and generates a unique insight based on its Dictionary blueprint:
* **Smart Tips:** It might remind you to prune a specific plant, warn you about bad neighbors, or suggest a fun culinary use.
* **Fun Facts:** There is a 25% chance the engine will magically override the standard tips to show you a custom Fun Fact or joke that your Admin added to a plant's dictionary entry!

> **Pro-Tip 💡**
> Plants with "Sweet" or "Spicy" flavor profiles have a much higher chance of being featured to inspire your dinner cooking!

### Interactive Features
* **Shuffle:** Not interested in the current tip? Tap the **Shuffle (🔀)** icon in the top corner to instantly generate a brand new insight.
* **Quick Navigation:** Tap the card itself to jump straight to that plant's detail profile!
