# Needs Watering & Hungry Plants

Your daily to-do lists! These simple queues show you exactly which individual plants are begging for a drink or a snack. The Hungry queue will even remind you exactly what kind of fertilizer each plant prefers!

### The Location Auditor
If you've assigned a specific Nutrient Profile to a Location (like dedicating a raised bed strictly to "Acid Lovers" for your blueberries), the Hungry Plants widget acts as your built-in auditor to prevent nutrient lock-out or root burn. 
If it spots a hungry plant sitting in a location that doesn't match its preferred diet (like trying to put a delicate Mediterranean herb in that acidic bed), it will pop up a bright red "Suboptimal Location" warning badge. It will even automatically scan your database and suggest the name of a better shelf to move the plant to!
