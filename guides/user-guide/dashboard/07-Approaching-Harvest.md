# Approaching Harvest

The best part of gardening! FloraSync automatically scans your planting dates and counts down the days until your delicious veggies are ready to pick or your beautiful flowers are expected to bloom.

### How it Works
FloraSync calculates exactly when a plant is ready by taking the day you planted it and adding the "Days to Harvest" rule from its Dictionary blueprint. To keep your dashboard clean, this widget stays completely hidden until there's actually something to pick!

* **The 14-Day Warning:** Once a plant gets within two weeks of its target harvest date, it magically pops into this carousel with a blue countdown.
* **Ready to Pick:** Once the countdown hits zero, the text turns orange to let you know it's **"Ready to pick!"**
* **The Grace Period:** Forget to harvest? Don't worry! The plant will remain stuck in this carousel for an extra 14 days *after* its harvest date to remind you to go grab it before it goes bad!

> **Pro-Tip 💡**
> Tap any card in the carousel to instantly jump to that plant's profile, where you can use the Journal to log the exact weight or quantity of your harvest!
