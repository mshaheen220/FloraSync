# Health Watchlist

Keep a close eye on the sick bay! If you spot aphids or a nasty fungus, FloraSync will automatically pin that plant right here on your dashboard so you don't forget to nurse it back to health.

### Flagging an Issue
When you add a journal entry to a plant, there is a field for "Health Issues." If you type anything into this box (like "Aphids", "Wilting", or "Powdery Mildew"), that plant is instantly pinned to your dashboard! The card will display the specific issue and count how many days it's been since you first reported it.

> **Pro-Tip 💡**
> The widget intelligently sorts itself to put the oldest, most ignored issues first, so no plant gets left behind!

### Resolving an Issue
Because the watchlist only looks at the *most recent* journal entry for a plant, clearing the alert is super easy. Once you've treated the problem, simply add a brand new Journal Entry for that plant and set the Health Issues field to **"None"**, **"Healthy"**, or **"Resolved"**. It will magically drop off your watchlist!
