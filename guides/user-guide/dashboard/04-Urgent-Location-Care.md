# Urgent Location Care

Got a whole shelf of drooping plants? This smart widget groups thirsty and hungry plants by their physical location. Instead of tapping a dozen individual plants, you get a magical single button to instantly "Water all on Shelf A"!

If you've assigned a specific Nutrient Profile to that location, the feed button will dynamically update to show you exactly what to mix (e.g., "Apply Leafy & Lush to Shelf A"). Tapping this button instantly logs that specific high-nitrogen feed for every single plant on the shelf, ensuring the Smart Math engine perfectly resets all their timers without you needing to answer any extra prompts!
