# 💡 Widget: Random Spotlight

The **Random Spotlight** is a dynamic, rotating card that surfaces interesting insights, tips, and trivia about the exact plants currently growing in your garden. 

## How It Works
Every time you load the dashboard, the engine selects a plant from your active inventory and generates a unique insight based on its Dictionary definition. 
*   **Smart Tips:** It might remind you to prune a specific plant, warn you about bad companion plants, or suggest a culinary use for an herb. *(Pro-Tip: Plants with 'Sweet' or 'Spicy' flavor profiles have a higher chance of being featured to inspire your dinner cooking!)*
*   **Fun Facts:** There is a 25% chance the engine will override the standard tips to show you a custom Fun Fact or Joke that was added to a plant's dictionary entry!

## Interactions
*   **Shuffle:** Tap the **Shuffle (🔀)** icon in the top right corner of the widget to instantly generate a brand new insight.
*   **Navigate:** Tap the card itself to jump straight to that plant's detail page.
