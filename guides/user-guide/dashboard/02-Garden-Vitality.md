# Garden Vitality

Get the big picture at a glance! This handy widget gives you a quick snapshot of your entire garden's overall health, showing you exactly how hydrated and well-fed your plants are, along with a live count of everything you're currently growing.

### Hydration & Nutrition
These twin meters show the average water and food levels across your whole garden. Keep an eye out—if either percentage drops to **30% or below**, the text will turn orange to warn you that a large portion of your garden is getting hungry or thirsty!

### Active Plants
This card shows exactly how many living, monitored plants you are currently caring for, compared to everything mapped in your history. **Tap this card** as a super-fast shortcut to instantly open your full Inventory!

### Top Zone
If you've organized your plants into physical Zones, this card displays your most heavily populated area. **Tap this card** to instantly jump to that Zone's profile and see everything growing inside it!
