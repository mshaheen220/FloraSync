# 📖 Widget: Garden Pulse

The **Garden Pulse** widget sits at the bottom of your Dashboard and provides a running, real-time feed of the 5 most recent activities that occurred across your entire garden. 

This is incredibly useful if you share your garden with a Helper or family member, as you can instantly see who watered or harvested what!

## Smart Batching
If you use a Quick Action (like "Water Greenhouse" or "Feed All"), FloraSync won't spam your Pulse feed with 50 individual entries. Instead, the engine intelligently groups simultaneous actions together and displays them as a single, clean bulk entry (e.g., *"Michael watered the Greenhouse location"*).

## Quick Navigation
Every item in the Garden Pulse feed is interactive. Tapping an entry will instantly jump you to the specific Plant, Location, or Zone that was acted upon, allowing you to quickly read the full journal notes or inspect the area!
