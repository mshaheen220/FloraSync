# Local Weather & Soil Insights (Opt-In Addon)

Want to connect your garden to the sky? While FloraSync is built to be a fully local, offline-first app that respects your privacy, it comes with an official, opt-in "Weather Widget" plugin! Once your Admin activates it, it securely connects to the **Open-Meteo** service to provide hyper-local weather conditions, exact soil temperatures, water evaporation rates, and impending storm alerts. It translates raw weather data into smart, actionable gardening advice right on your screen!

### The Smart Garden Outlook
Instead of making you guess what the weather means, FloraSync automatically synthesizes the raw data into a single, color-coded **Garden Outlook** summary. If heavy rain is coming, or if the soil is getting dangerously dry, it will warn you right on the main card!

### Dive Deeper (Expandable Stats)
Want the exact numbers? Tap the **Garden Outlook** card to instantly expand the widget and reveal a detailed grid of 8 crucial gardening metrics:
* **Soil Health:** Soil Temperature & Soil Moisture.
* **Water Loss:** Solar Radiation & Evapotranspiration (how fast plants are sweating).
* **Atmosphere:** Humidity & Wind Speed.
* **Forecast:** Rain Volume & Precipitation Outlook.

### Educational Tags & Details
Every single metric comes with a color-coded **Warning Tag** (like "Rot Risk" for high humidity, or "Optimal" for perfect soil temps) so you instantly know if something is wrong. 

> **Pro-Tip 💡**
> Don't know what "Evapotranspiration" means? Just tap on any of the 8 metric cards! A detailed window will pop up explaining exactly what that metric means for your plants, alongside a personalized status recommendation based on the current data.
