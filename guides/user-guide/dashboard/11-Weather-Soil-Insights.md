# Local Weather Insights (Opt-In Addon)

Want to connect your garden to the sky? While FloraSync is built to be a fully local, offline-first app that respects your privacy, it comes with an official, opt-in "Weather Widget" plugin! Once your Admin activates it, it securely connects to the **Tomorrow.io** service to provide hyper-local weather conditions, UV Index, cloud cover, and impending storm alerts. It translates raw weather data into smart, actionable gardening advice right on your screen!

### The Smart Garden Outlook
Instead of making you guess what the weather means, FloraSync automatically synthesizes the raw data into a single, color-coded **Garden Outlook** summary. If heavy rain is coming, or if the wind is getting dangerously strong, it will warn you right on the main card!

### Dive Deeper (Expandable Stats)
Want the exact numbers? Tap the **Garden Outlook** card to instantly expand the widget and reveal a detailed grid of crucial gardening metrics:
* **Atmosphere:** Humidity, Wind Speed, and Cloud Cover.
* **Sunlight:** Solar Radiation & UV Index.
* **Forecast:** Rain Volume & Precipitation Outlook.
* **Live Sync:** A dedicated "Updated" panel showing the last time data was synced, complete with a manual refresh button!

> **Note 📝**
> To save your API limits, the widget automatically caches weather data for 15 minutes. If you want to force a fresh pull, just tap the refresh icon in the bottom right corner of the grid!

### Educational Tags & Details
Every single metric comes with a color-coded **Warning Tag** (like "Rot Risk" for high humidity, or "Extreme" for high UV) so you instantly know if something is wrong. 

> **Pro-Tip 💡**
> Don't know what "UV Index" or "Solar Radiation" means? Just tap on any of the metric cards! A detailed window will pop up explaining exactly what that metric means for your plants, alongside a personalized status recommendation and action item based on the current data.
