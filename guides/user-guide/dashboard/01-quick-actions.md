# ⚡ Widget: Quick Actions

The **Quick Actions** widget sits at the top of your Dashboard and is designed to save you time. Instead of scanning tags or clicking through menus one by one, you can execute bulk commands across your entire garden with a single tap.

*(Note: You must be an Owner or Helper to use Quick Actions. Viewers will not see this widget!)*

## Default Actions
By default, you will always have access to **Water All** and **Feed All**. Tapping these will instantly reset the hydration or nutrition meters for *every tracked plant* in your inventory to 100%, and generate a detailed bulk journal entry.

## Customizing with Pinned Buttons (📌)
The real power of Quick Actions comes from customizing it to fit your workflow. You can create your own shortcut buttons to live right on the Dashboard!

When viewing the detail page for any **Zone**, **Location**, or individual **Plant**, tap the **Pin (📌)** icon in the top corner. You can choose to pin a specific action to your dashboard:
*   **Water / Feed Shortcuts:** Creates a button to instantly water/feed just that specific Zone or Location (e.g., a "Water Greenhouse" button).
*   **Navigation Shortcuts:** Creates a quick-link button that instantly jumps you to that item's detail page without having to search for it.
