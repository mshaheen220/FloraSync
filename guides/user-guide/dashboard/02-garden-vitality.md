# 📊 Widget: Garden Vitality

The **Garden Vitality** widget provides a high-level health check of your entire digital greenhouse at a single glance. It aggregates the data from all of your tracked plants into simple, actionable metrics.

## Hydration & Nutrition Percentages
These twin meters show the average water and food levels across your whole garden. If either percentage drops to **30% or below**, the text will turn orange to warn you that a large portion of your garden is getting hungry or thirsty!

## Active Plants
This card shows exactly how many living, monitored plants you are currently caring for, compared against the total number of items mapped in your inventory (which includes harvested/historical plants). **Tap this card** as a quick shortcut to open your full Inventory Manager!

## Top Zone
If you have organized your plants into physical Zones, this card displays your most heavily populated area. **Tap this card** to instantly jump to that Zone's detail page and see everything growing inside it.
