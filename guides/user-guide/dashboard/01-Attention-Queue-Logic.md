# Attention Queue Logic

Behind the scenes, FloraSync acts like your personal garden assistant! Instead of giving you a static, overwhelming list to scroll through, it constantly calculates exactly how thirsty or hungry each plant is. It does this by comparing the last time you watered it against its specific needs, and even factors in whether it's sitting in the hot sun or a humid greenhouse. Plants that are overdue for care are automatically floated straight to the very top of your queues!
