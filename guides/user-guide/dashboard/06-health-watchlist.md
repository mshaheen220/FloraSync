# 🚨 Widget: Health Watchlist

The **Health Watchlist** is an urgent alert carousel that ensures sick or pest-infested plants are never forgotten. It pulls its data directly from your Plant Journals.

## Flagging an Issue
When you add a journal entry to a plant, there is a field for "Health Issues." If you log anything in this box (e.g., "Aphids," "Wilting," or "Powdery Mildew"), that plant is instantly pinned to the Health Watchlist on your Dashboard, displaying the specific issue and how many days ago it was reported.

*(Note: The widget intelligently sorts itself to put the oldest, most ignored issues first!)*

## Resolving an Issue
Because the watchlist only looks at the *most recent* journal entry for a plant, clearing the alert is easy. Once you've treated the problem, simply add a new Journal Entry for that plant and set the Health Issues field to **"None"**, **"Healthy"**, or **"Resolved"**.
