# Inventory & Plant Tracking

The Inventory view is the heart of your active garden tracking! It displays all the living plants currently growing in your physical spaces, keeping you perfectly up to date on what needs your attention.

* **Smart Grouping & Search:** Finding your plants is a breeze. By default, plants are grouped by their Category (like 'Vegetables' or 'Herbs'), but you can dynamically re-group them by their Zone or specific Location. Looking for that one specific tomato? Just start typing in the live search bar, and the relevant groups will automatically pop open!
* **Dynamic Hydration Math:** Wondering how FloraSync knows when a plant is thirsty? We do the heavy lifting for you! Each plant calculates a real-time hydration score. It looks at the baseline watering rules from the Plant Dictionary, adjusts for the plant's sun exposure (e.g., "Full Sun" dries out 20% faster), and even factors in the custom environment of the Zone it's living in.
* **Plant Profiles:** Tapping a plant card—or scanning its physical QR code—opens its dedicated profile. Here's what you'll find:
  * **Vitality Status:** Quick-glance visual badges tell you exactly how your plant is doing. Is it perfectly hydrated? Begging for water? Or is it marked as "Unmonitored / Rain-Fed" (which gracefully hides established outdoor shrubs or trees from your daily to-do lists)?
  * **Milestone Tracking:** FloraSync automatically estimates upcoming bloom or harvest dates based on when you planted it. For perennials and biennials, it smartly rolls the anniversary forward year after year!
  * **Cultivation Tips:** Get instant access to care basics, traits, and lifecycle instructions inherited directly from the global Plant Dictionary, so you always know exactly what your plant needs.
  * **Quick Actions:** Log watering or feeding events with a single tap! If your workspace uses any Addon Plugins, their special action buttons will show up right here, too.
  * **Trickle-Down Journal:** A complete timeline of your plant's life. It seamlessly blends specific notes you've written about this plant with macro-events inherited from its parent Zone (like when you logged "Watered the Greenhouse").
