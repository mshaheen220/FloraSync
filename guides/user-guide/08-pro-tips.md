# 💡 Pro-Tips

### Filter the Journal Noise
Looking for that specific seedling photo you took last month? Open your Master Journal and toggle off **"Show Routine Care"** to instantly hide all the daily watering and feeding logs. This leaves a clean, beautiful timeline of just your notes, photos, and major milestones!

### Zero-Click Chores
Want to water your whole greenhouse in one second? Print a special "Action Tag" (like *Water Greenhouse*) from the Print Center and stick it next to the door. Scan it on your way out, and FloraSync instantly logs a watering event for every single plant inside without you ever having to tap your screen!

### Just-In-Time Planting
Don't want to bring your laptop into the dirt? Keep a stack of printed, unassigned blank QR tags in your garden shed. When you drop a new seed into a pot, just slap a blank tag on it and scan it with your phone. FloraSync will instantly pop open a quick-add menu so you can register your new plant on the spot.

### Unmonitored / Rain-Fed Plants
Got a mature rhododendron in the front yard that you want in your inventory, but you don't want it begging for water every 4 days? Edit the plant and check the **"Unmonitored / Rain-fed"** box. It will gracefully drop out of your daily care queues and no longer affect your Garden Vitality score, but you can still use its personal journal to track when it blooms!

### Inventory Pivot
Inside your **Inventory**, use the "Group By" dropdown at the top to instantly pivot your master list. Viewing your plants by "Category" is great for seeing all your Herbs at once, while viewing by "Zone" gives you a perfect physical map of your yard.

### Fast Searching
Both the Dictionary and the Inventory Manager have a live search bar. Don't bother opening folders—just start typing the name of the plant you're looking for, and the relevant accordions will automatically spring open!

### Switching Gardens (Workspaces)
If you help manage multiple gardens (like a community greenhouse and your home patio), you can easily jump between them. Tap your current garden's name (or logo) at the top left of the **Dashboard** or **Settings** screen to open the Workspace Switcher, then tap the garden you want to switch to.
