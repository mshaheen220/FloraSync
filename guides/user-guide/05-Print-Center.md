# Print Center & QR Codes

The Print Center is the magic bridge between your phone and your physical garden! It generates perfectly spaced, ready-to-print sheets of QR codes so you can tag your plants, shelves, and garden beds.

### Three Ways to Print
* **The Print Queue:** As you wander through your digital garden, you can easily queue up individual plants or spaces to be printed later—perfect for replacing a faded tag or labeling a fresh cutting. Don't want to waste half a sheet of sticker paper? Tap **Smart Fill**, and FloraSync will automatically fill the empty spaces on your page with sequential blank tags!
* **Bulk Print:** Just starting out? Instantly generate a massive sheet of QR codes for every single living plant and location you've logged in your database.
* **Blank Sequences:** Print an entire sheet of unassigned, sequentially numbered blank tags (like `TAG-001`, `TAG-002`). You can peel and stick these onto empty pots right now. Later, when you finally drop a seed in the dirt, just scan the blank tag with your phone to instantly name and register the new plant!

### Label Formats & Zero-Click Actions
The Print Center supports multiple physical sticker sizes right out of the box, from massive 10x6cm garden stakes down to tiny 1-inch square micro-stickers.

**The Best Part: Zero-Click Chores!**
You can embed magical "Zero-Click" actions directly into your printed QR codes. For example, you can print a standard "Info" tag for your Greenhouse, and stick a special "Water Greenhouse" tag right next to it. Scanning that second tag will instantly log a watering event for *every single plant* inside the greenhouse—you won't even need to tap your screen!
