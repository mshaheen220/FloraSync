# Appearance & Theming

FloraSync offers a gorgeous, highly customizable interface designed to look great in any environment. The best part? These settings are tied exclusively to your personal profile, so go ahead and get creative—changing your colors won't mess up what your garden helpers see on their screens!
* **🌙 Display Mode:** Choose between Light and Dark mode, or select **System Default** to let FloraSync automatically match your phone. This ensures your screen is always easy to read, whether you're standing in a glaringly bright greenhouse or doing a midnight pest check!
* **🖌️ Color Themes:** Make your workspace yours! Choose from a variety of beautiful, garden-inspired color palettes to update your buttons, navigation, and accents. *(Hint: You can even unlock wild new color themes by installing Plugins!)*
* **🎭 Icon Styles:** You can completely change the icons used throughout the app to match your garden's exact vibe. This instantly updates the look of the water, feed, and edit buttons everywhere!
