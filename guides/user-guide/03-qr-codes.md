# 📷 The Physical Bridge: QR Codes

The magic of FloraSync lies in the physical QR tags placed in your pots and beds. Tap the floating **Camera Button (📷)** on the Dashboard to open the scanner.

*!Screenshot: The in-app camera scanner targeting a physical QR code on a plant stake.*

## Printing Tags (The Print Center)
You can generate and download perfectly formatted sheets of QR codes directly from the app!
1. Open the Hamburger Menu and go to **Settings**.
2. Scroll to the **Print Center**.
3. Choose **Database Export** to print tags with names for everything currently in your system, or **Blank Tags** to print unassigned stickers.
4. **Print Queue & Smart Fill:** Tap the 🖨️ buttons on any Plant, Location, or Zone detail screen to add specific tags to your Print Queue. When you print your queue, turn on **Smart Fill Sheet** to automatically generate blank tags for any remaining empty spaces on the paper!
5. Click **Generate** and download your PNG sheets!

> **DIY Weatherproofing Tip 🌧️**
> To make your labels survive the elements, print them onto **full-sheet vinyl sticker paper**, then laminate the entire printed sheet. The laminating plastic will adhere to both sides. When you cut out your individual tags, simply peel the back lamination layer off to expose the vinyl's sticky back and apply it to your stakes!

## "Zero-Click" Care
There are two types of tags: **Navigation Tags** and **Action Tags**. A Navigation tag simply loads up the details of a plant or zone.
If you generate a QR code with an Action built-in (e.g., a "Water Me" 💧 or "Feed Me" 🍽️ tag), simply scanning that tag instantly updates the database and resets the hydration bar to 100%. No buttons to press, no forms to fill out!

## Just-In-Time (JIT) Registration
When you print a sheet of blank, unassigned tags, you don't need to register them on your computer first. 
1. Stick a blank tag in a new pot.
2. Scan it with your phone.
3. FloraSync will say, *"New Tag Detected! What are we planting here?"*
4. Pick a plant from your dictionary, assign it to a location, and you're done!
