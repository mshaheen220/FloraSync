# 🎨 Personalizing Your Experience: Appearance Settings

FloraSync allows every user to customize how the application looks and feels. Because these settings are tied to your specific user profile, changing them won't affect what your collaborators see!

Navigate to the **Settings** screen via the Hamburger Menu, and tap **Appearance** to access your personalization options.

## 🌙 Display Mode (Light / Dark)
You can force the application into **Light Mode** or **Dark Mode**, or select **System Default**. If you choose System, FloraSync will automatically switch its interface to match your phone or computer's current OS settings (perfect for when the sun goes down!).

## 🖌️ Color Themes
FloraSync features a variety of beautiful, garden-inspired color palettes. Changing your Color Theme will update the primary buttons, navigation accents, and active states throughout the entire application. *Note: New custom color themes can also be added dynamically via Plugins!*
