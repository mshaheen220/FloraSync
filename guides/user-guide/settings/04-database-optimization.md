# 🚀 Database Optimization

Have you snapped way too many high-resolution photos of your prized tomatoes? Because FloraSync is completely local, saving hundreds of massive 12-megapixel photos can eventually slow down your network syncing.

We built a magic button for that! 

In the Data Import & Optimization section, tap the **Optimize Database** button. FloraSync will safely scan your entire garden history (plants, journals, locations, and zones) and permanently compress any oversized photos. Your images will still look beautiful on your screen, but your app will stay lightning fast!
