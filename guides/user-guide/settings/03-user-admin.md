# 👥 User Administration

Gardening is better together! If you are a Garden Owner or Admin, this section gives you the power to invite friends, family, or garden-sitters to your workspace. 

### How to Manage Your Team
* **Inviting a Helper:** Adding someone new is a breeze! Just tap **Add User**, enter their details, and select their role. They will instantly have access to your garden the next time they log in.
* **Changing Roles:** Did your garden-sitter prove they have a green thumb? You can easily upgrade their role from Helper to Admin at any time, or downgrade them to Viewer if you need to restrict their access.
* **Removing Access:** If someone moves away, you can instantly revoke their access with a single tap, keeping your garden data perfectly safe and secure.

### Garden Roles
When you add someone to your garden, you can assign them a specific role to keep your data perfectly safe:
* **Admin:** Your trusted right hand. Admins can do almost everything you can, including adding new plants to the Dictionary, managing other users, and editing the garden profile.
* **Helper:** Perfect for garden-sitters! Helpers can log chores (like watering or feeding) and add fun notes to the journal, but they cannot accidentally delete plants, edit your master dictionary, or mess with your settings.
* **Viewer:** Read-only mode. Viewers can explore your garden, read the journal, and enjoy your photos, but they cannot click any action buttons or make any changes!

### Who's the Boss?
While **Admins** have a lot of power, every garden has exactly one **Owner**. The Owner is the ultimate boss of the workspace and is the only person who can permanently delete the garden or configure Addon Plugins!

### Looking for your other gardens?
This section is strictly for managing the team in your *current* workspace. If you want to see a list of all the different gardens you are a member of (or switch to a different one), simply tap the garden name at the top left of your screen to open the **Workspace Switcher**!
