# 👤 Account Info

Your personal hub! This section is entirely yours and manages how you interact with FloraSync.

* **Profile Avatar:** Make your mark! Tap the camera icon on your profile picture to upload a custom avatar. If you don't set one, we'll give you a colorful initial based on your name.
* **Display Name:** Update your name so your garden helpers know exactly who is leaving notes in the Master Journal. 
> **Note 📝** 
> Your core Login Username cannot be changed here
* **Permissions:** Check your current System Role and Garden Access Level to instantly understand what tools and buttons are available to you in the current workspace.
* **Security:** Need a new password? Tap **Change Password** to securely update your login credentials.
* **Active Session:** Use the **Log Out** button to safely sign out of your current session on this device.
