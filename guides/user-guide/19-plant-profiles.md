# 🌿 Plant Profiles

Tapping on any plant in your Inventory or on the Dashboard will open its **Plant Profile**. This is the dedicated command center for that specific, individual plant.

## The Action Strip & Pinning
Right below the plant's photo and basic stats, you will find the Action Strip. This allows you to instantly log a watering or feeding event.

If you find yourself watering this specific plant very often, you can tap the **Pin (📌)** icon to create a dedicated shortcut button for it right on your main Dashboard!

## Editing the Plant
By tapping the **Edit (✏️)** button in the top corner, you can modify the plant's current state:
*   **Move it:** Did you move a potted plant from the Patio to the Greenhouse? Update its Zone and Location here so the app knows to apply the correct evaporation modifiers!
*   **Mark as Unmonitored:** Check the "Unmonitored / Rain-Fed" box to drop the plant out of your daily care queues. It will no longer beg for water, but you can still track it in your inventory!
*   **Set Custom Thumbnail:** While the plant will inherit a default photo from the Dictionary, you can upload a custom cover photo to easily identify it.

## Printing a Tag
Tap the **Print (🖨️)** button to instantly add this specific plant to your Print Queue. When you navigate to the Print Center later, this plant will be waiting to be printed on a physical QR sticker!
