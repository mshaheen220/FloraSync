# 📋 The Inventory Manager

The Inventory Manager is your master list of every active, living plant currently tracked in your garden. You can access it by opening the Hamburger Menu and tapping **Inventory**.

## Sorting & Organization
At the top of the screen, you will find a **"Group By"** dropdown. This is a powerful tool to instantly pivot how you view your garden:
*   **Group by Zone:** Sorts your master list into physical locations, providing a perfect layout map of your yard.
*   **Group by Category:** Clusters your inventory by type (e.g., all Vegetables together, all Herbs together).
*   **Group by None:** Displays a standard alphabetical list of everything you own.

## Searching
If you have hundreds of plants, use the **Search Bar** right below the grouping dropdown. As you type, FloraSync will instantly filter the list and automatically expand the relevant groups so your result pops right onto the screen!

## Adding New Plants
To register a brand new plant, tap the **+ Add Plant** button at the very bottom of the Inventory list. This will walk you through assigning it an Archetype from the dictionary and placing it in a specific Location in your garden.
