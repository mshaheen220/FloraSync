# 📖 The Plant Dictionary (Archetypes)

Before you can plant something, it needs to exist in your Dictionary. This is where you define the "baseline" rules for a specific species or strain.

*!Screenshot: The Plant Dictionary screen showing the accordion list of herbs and vegetables.*

Navigate to the **Plant Dictionary** via the Hamburger Menu. Here, you set the immutable traits of a crop:
*   **Care Intervals:** How many days between watering and feeding?
*   **Sunlight:** Full sun, part shade, or full shade?
*   **Lifecycle:** Annual, Biennial, or Perennial?
*   **Harvest:** How many days until it's ready to pick?

Once a plant is in the dictionary, you can spawn infinite physical *Instances* of it in your garden, and they will all inherit these rules.

## Shared by Everyone
The dictionary is global and shared across all gardens and workspaces on your server. To keep this master reference clean and accurate, only users with the **Owner** or **God-Admin** role can add new plants or edit existing entries. Helpers and Viewers can view the dictionary and plant from it, but cannot alter the global rules.

## Privacy & Your Data
FloraSync is a local, privacy-first command center. It **does not use the internet** to "research" plants or pull from public databases. 

The Plant Dictionary is built entirely from your own private data. When you add a plant and define its rules, you are teaching your local server exactly how you want your garden managed. Your data never leaves your network!
