# 🌍 Personalizing Your Command Center

The **FloraSync Dashboard** is designed to adapt entirely to your personal gardening workflow. Instead of forcing you to look at a static page, the modular widget system lets you prioritize the queues, metrics, and insights you care about most—while hiding sections you aren't using to keep your workspace clean and tidy.

[Screenshot: The main Dashboard view scrolled all the way to the bottom, highlighting the 'Customize Dashboard' button right below the Garden Pulse widget] 

### Tailoring Your Widget Layout

Because personalization settings are saved directly to your unique user profile, arranging your layout on one device will automatically sync across all your other screens without messing up the view for your garden collaborators.

**Open the Personalization Suite**
Scroll past your active cards all the way to the very bottom of the Dashboard page. Tap the **Customize Dashboard** button to expand the configuration tray. The button text will instantly change to **Done Customizing**.

[Screenshot: The Visible Sections customization tray opened, revealing the list of dashboard widgets equipped with checkboxes on the left and drag handles on the right] 

**Reorder Your Layout Priority**
Locate the widget you want to shift within the **Visible Sections** list. Press, hold, and drag the handle icon (represented by the vertical dots) on the right side of the widget card to slide it higher or lower in the stack. For example, dragging **Garden Pulse** upward lets you position your recent activity feed right below your top widgets.

**Toggle Widget Visibility Preferences**
To declutter your feed, use the checkboxes on the left side of the widget names. Unchecking a box, such as turning off **The Nursery**, will instantly drop that carousel from your view so you can focus on other priorities. Conversely, checking an inactive box like **Urgent Location Care** will immediately inject it back into your active dashboard configuration.

**Save Your Changes and Sync**
Once you are happy with your new setup, scroll back up slightly and tap the **Done Customizing** button. This closes the tray and writes your layout preferences directly to your profile database, ensuring your personalized view seamlessly follows you to any device you log into next!
