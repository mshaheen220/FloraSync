# 🌱 Widget: The Nursery

The **Nursery** widget is a smart, horizontal carousel that highlights the youngest, most vulnerable additions to your garden. Like the Approaching Harvest widget, it remains hidden to save space and only appears when relevant.

## How It Works
FloraSync looks at the `Date Planted` value for every active plant in your inventory. If a plant was registered within the **last 14 days**, it will appear in the Nursery. 

This serves as a visual reminder to keep a close eye on fresh seedlings or newly transplanted crops that might be susceptible to root shock or sudden dry spells. Once a plant has survived in the garden for two weeks, it automatically graduates and drops out of the Nursery carousel!
