# 🌍 The Command Center (Dashboard)

The Dashboard is the heart of FloraSync. Instead of a standard static list, it acts as a dynamic sorting engine that surfaces the most urgent tasks and relevant insights using a modular widget system.

*!Screenshot: The Dashboard showing Quick Actions, Garden Vitality, and the Needs Watering queue.*

At the top of your Dashboard, you will always find your **Quick Actions** and **Garden Vitality** statistics. Below that, FloraSync displays a variety of smart widgets by default:

*   **Random Spotlight**
*   **Approaching Harvest**
*   **The Nursery**
*   **Health Watchlist**
*   **Urgent Location Care**
*   **Needs Watering**
*   **Hungry Plants**
*   **Garden Pulse**

## Customizing Your Dashboard

You have full control over how your command center is organized. Scroll to the very bottom of the Dashboard and tap **Customize Dashboard**.

*   **Reordering:** Use the up/down arrows, or grab the drag handle (vertical dots) to slide widgets up and down to change their priority.
*   **Hiding Widgets:** Uncheck the box next to any widget you don't want to see. You can always turn it back on later!
*   **Saved Preferences:** Your layout preferences are automatically saved to your specific user profile. If you log in on another device, your customized dashboard layout will follow you!
